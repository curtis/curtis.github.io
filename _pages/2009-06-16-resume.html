Summary

I’m a Phoenix-based Ruby on Rails developer and co-founder of Velocity Labs, a Ruby on Rails ewb application development company.

I have a MS in Computer Science and have written software professionally since 2001. I've worked at small agile companies and large bureaucratic companies and found that my true passion lies in working in and around startup companies. With Flatterline, I get a chance everyday to help Phoenix startups realize their vision.

While I work mostly in Ruby on Rails, I'm also expanding my skill set into iPhone development as that is something often requested by the startups with which we work.

    Founder (2009 – Present)

    Flatterline is a kick ass web development company in Phoenix, AZ that specializes in helping early stage startups. We work primarily as Ruby on Rails developers.
