I have a new favorite application for the Mac called Delicious Library. It is an application for cataloging, managing, and sharing your books, music, games, and movies. It integrates with iCal and the AddressBook to make thing easier. It also uses your iSight to scan the bar code on the back of the media, how cool is that?

Every time you buy an item through Delicious Library, Delicious Monster donates 100% of the revenue sharing that it gets from Amazon.com (5-7%) to various charities around the world.

Awesome!
