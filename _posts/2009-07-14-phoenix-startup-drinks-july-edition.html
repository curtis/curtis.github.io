After talking with quite a few people, we’ve settled on having the first Phoenix Startup Drinks in Tempe at Four Peaks Brewery!

When

2nd Tues: Tuesday, July 14, 2009 from 6-9pm
Last Thurs: Thursday, July 30, 2009 from 6-9pm

We’re trying to have it twice a month on different days to accommodate more people. If this format doesn’t work, then we’ll change it and let you know :)

About the venue

Housed in a historic brick building dating to 1892, Four Peaks Brewery is a staple of Tempe nightlife. It’s won many awards over the years for it’s unique beers and was recently awarded the 2009 Critics’ Pick for Best Local Brewery from AZCentral as well as the 2009 Readers’ Choice for Best Pub with Grub.
